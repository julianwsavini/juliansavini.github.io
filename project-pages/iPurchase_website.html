Overview

During my Co-Op with ISS Group, I was deeply involved in redesigning one of their flagship frontend products, iPurchase. The project aimed to modernize and enhance user interaction by leveraging Material Design Bootstrap (MDBootstrap) to create a responsive and dynamic interface.

See UI Here

Project Highlights

  • Implemented dynamic field labels and tab-to-accordion transformations to optimize usability on various screen sizes.
  • Designed responsive tables that adapt layouts based on screen dimensions, ensuring compatibility with over 10 device types.
  • Collaborated with cross-functional teams and stakeholders to iterate on design prototypes, striving for continuous improvement.
  • Delivered a frontend that consisted of 5000+ lines of code, applying modern UX principles for a seamless user experience.
  • Achieved a 50% higher user satisfaction rate through A/B testing and iterative design enhancements.

Technical Achievements

I developed the iPurchase frontend using a robust tech stack, focusing on responsive design, performance, and usability.

Tech Stack:

  • HTML
  • CSS (MDBootstrap)
  • JavaScript

The frontend leveraged MDBootstrap's prebuilt components for a professional look while maintaining flexibility for customizations. I integrated features like collapsible navigation menus and interactive data tables to meet user requirements.

UX Research & Critical Thinking

Beyond design, I conducted extensive UX research, engaging directly with clients to gather feedback and insights. This user-centric approach uncovered pain points and guided improvements in the interface.

My ability to critically evaluate each iteration ensured that the final product surpassed expectations. By balancing creativity and analytical thinking, I helped transform iPurchase into a powerful tool tailored to user needs.

Reflections

This experience was a cornerstone of my professional growth. It taught me the importance of iterative design and the value of stakeholder collaboration. Working part-time until May, I focused on understanding client needs and refining the interface to align with user goals.

The emphasis on continuous improvement fostered a culture of excellence, and I am immensely proud of the contributions I made to this transformative project.
